About Azumanga Daioh

Azumanga Daioh is a slice-of-life comedy anime and manga that follows the daily lives of a group of high school girls and their quirky teachers.

Main Characters

    Chiyo Mihama - The child prodigy

    Grade skipping: Chiyo skipped five grades and started high school in 10th grade. This is unusual in Japan, and the series explores the consequences of her skipping grades. Class representative: Chiyo was chosen by her classmates to be the class representative. Small stature: Chiyo is small, which makes her feel self-conscious, especially in sports. Wealthy family: Chiyo's family is very wealthy, owning a large mansion and summer house. Pigtails: Chiyo's pigtails are a source of visual gags in the series. Osaka often imagines them as wings, or as entities that are independent of Chiyo. Great Pyrenees dog: Chiyo's dog is named Mr. Tadakichi, and she often rides on his back. Relationships Chiyo is friendly with Osaka, who is her closest friend in class. Tomo is the only classmate who seems to enjoy teasing Chiyo.

    Mr. Tadakichi - Chiyo's dog

    Mr. Tadakichi is Chiyo's pet dog He is a Great Pyrenees He is gentle and mild-mannered Chiyo can ride on his back Sakaki likes him because he allows her to pet him without biting her. Mr. Tadakichi has been with Chiyo for five years before she started high school Sakaki daydreams about riding Mr. Tadakichi like Chiyo does

    Osaka - the lifelong dreamer

    Osaka is easy-going and daydreams often She has a unique perspective and appreciates the little things She lives life at her own pace and doesn't try to change herself for others She seems slow and easily distracted, but her mind works differently than most people She has difficulty focusing on schoolwork and gets poor grades She has trouble with sports, like the bread-eating race at the Sports Festival Other characteristics She has a hard time touching her toes She considers her label "Osaka" "too simple" and "icky" She lacks the energy or motivation to dispute her label

    Sakaki - The animal lover

    shy, sensitive, kind, polite, compassionate, gentle, intelligent, and academically gifted. Interests- Animals, especially cats Cute things Friendship Relationships- Befriends Chiyo after discovering that Chiyo's dog allows her to pet him The subject of Kaorin's infatuation Misunderstandings- Classmates think she's mysterious, cool, and doesn't like cute things Classmates think she's aloof and surly Goals- Befriend the stray cats in her neighborhood Be more like Chiyo, who wears her feelings on her sleeve

    Tomo Takino - The energetic troublemaker

    Energetic: Tomo is always full of energy and enthusiasm. Competitive: Tomo is competitive and often challenges people to competitions she can't win. Impulsive: Tomo is impulsive and doesn't consider the consequences of her actions. Slacker: Tomo is a slacker who doesn't always try to reach her goals. Relationships: Yomi Tomo's classmate since elementary school, and the one she irritates the most. Tomo often teases Yomi about her weight. Chiyo Tomo is the only one of the main cast who picks on Chiyo. Tomo once threw Chiyo's house keys into the forest. Osaka and Kagura Tomo is the unofficial "leader" of the "Knuckleheads" along with Osaka and Kagura. Favorite foods: fried chicken, egg roll, meat-balls, and bananas.

    Yomi - The voice of reason

    Yomi is smart and athletic, but she is dissatisfied with her weight and constantly tries to lose it. She is often tempted by her favorite foods, and can't seem to stick to her diets. Yomi is an above-average speaker, but she is a poor singer. She is often Tomo's "straight man", but she can sometimes be cruel. She has an uppercut that she uses when Tomo pushes her too far. She uses more masculine Japanese speech patterns than most of her peers. Other traits Yomi is a grade-school friend of Tomo. She is the general antithesis of Tomo. She is sometimes bitter.

    Kagura - The sporty one

    Kagura is competitive with her peers, especially in sports. She is driven by competition but is also sweet and sensitive. Good-natured Kagura is eager to help people and is good-natured. She is often seen helping her classmates, such as scaring away cats from Sakaki. Obsessed with Sakaki Kagura has a slight obsession with Sakaki, whom she sees as an athletic rival. Academic performance: Kagura has low test scores and is not interested in studying. She is placed in the "Bonkuras" (translated as "Knuckleheads" in the anime and "Numbnuts" in the manga). Other characteristics: Kagura is often tan because she spends most of her time on the swim team. She has a respect for Nyamo that occasionally borders on hero-worship. How she joins Yukari's class: Yukari selects Kagura as a ringer to win the school athletic competitions.

    Kaorin - The shy admirer

    She is shy, sensitive, and prone to jealousy. She also has artistic talent and helped design Chiyo's penguin suit. Personality Shy and sensitive, Can be prone to jealousy, Can be neurotic, and Has artistic talent. Other traits Implied to be a closeted lesbian and Can display a ferocious temper. Role in the series Helped design Chiyo's penguin suit

    Chihiro - The supportive friend

    She is a classmate and friend of Kaorin. Little is known except her name. When she appears, she is usually keeping Kaorin company, and she collaborates with her on some projects. Once, when Chiyo-chan tutored her, Yukari assumed it was the reverse.

    Miss Yukari - The eccentric homeroom teacher

    Yukari has brown hair and brown eyes Her teaching outfit is a pink shirt, blue collared button-up shirt, and blue skirt When she's not teaching, she wears a light beige sweater with yellow, orange, and red details, and a red skirt Personality Yukari is selfish, irresponsible, and easily angered. She can be a horrible tyrant or one of the sweetest teachers. She can be mercilessly teasing or kind and gentle. She can be dysfunctional and have severe mood swings Behavior. She can hit students with blunt objects. She can give encouraging notes on tests. She can become jealous when her friend receives nicer birthday presents. She can give her students study hall so she can go swimming. Relationship with students: Her students are casual enough to call her by her given name. She has a close relationship with her class

    Miss "Nyamo" Kurosawa - The gym teacher

    She is a gym teacher at the girls' school. In the girls' first year of high school, she is the homeroom teacher of class 5. During their last two years, she is the homeroom teacher of class 2.

    Mr. Kimura - The creepy English teacher

    Kimura is a "creepy" and ephebophiliac Classical Japanese teacher, who openly admits he became a teacher to be close to high-school girls. He is constantly lewd and openly seeks excuses to "observe" girls, such as during swimming class or when they are measured and weighed by the school nurse.
